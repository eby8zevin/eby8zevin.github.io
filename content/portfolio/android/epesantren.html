ePesantren

Description

ePesantren is an online Islamic boarding school application that assists pesantrens in managing their administration. It covers student administration, employee management, academics, finance, financial reports, employee attendance, a mobile app for student guardians, and even sending announcements and bills through WhatsApp notifications.
