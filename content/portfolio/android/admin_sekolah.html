AdminSekolah

Description

  • as Mobile Developer

AdminSekolah is an online school administration system that helps schools manage their administration. It includes student administration, employee management, academics, finance, financial reports, employee attendance, a mobile app for parents, teaching journals, and even the ability to send announcements and bills through WhatsApp notifications.
